What Makes Great Content Great?



“Content” is really just information – really, simple as that! When you create content, you’re putting information on your site. Some of it is intended to teach, some of it is intended to persuade, some of it is intended to entertain. And great content is information that succeeds at teaching, persuading or entertaining your visitors while also helping you meet your business goals.
